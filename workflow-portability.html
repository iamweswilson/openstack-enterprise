Federated Identity

Simple security for Workload Portability Across OpenStack Clouds

Benefits of Using Federated Identity

Federated identity offers significant advantages to Digital Film Tree, Comcast, directors, producers, editors, and consumers, including:

  • Workflow acceleration: Prior to this approach, video was shipped on disk or tape via truck. Typically, this meant a simple editing or post-production effort took 2-3 days to do a review cycle. With OpenStack, their workflow now takes 2-3 hours.
  • Workflow simplification: In the past, final content was written to tape, and sent to Comcast on a truck. Now Digital Film Tree or one of their cloud providers flow the final video to Comcast’s private cloud – one OS cloud to another.
  • Enhanced security: Since Digital Film Tree acts as an identity provider, maintaining security and access is streamlined because they are the only party responsible for maintaining userid/password lists. Changes (new hires, resignations) are effective across every service provider instantaneously. Also, admins can add federation enabled external clouds as part of Digital Film Tree’s trust as needed, minimizing ad hoc reliance on untrusted public cloud resources.
  • Instant-on content delivery: Since Comcast Cable receives content to their own OpenStack cloud, they delivers TV shows to viewers in their living room directly from their cloud to set-tops cable boxes – minimizing complexity and strengthening reliability.
