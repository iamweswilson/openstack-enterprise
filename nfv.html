OpenStack Foundation Report

For global telecoms, the future is here today with OpenStack

OpenStack is the choice as the foundation for NFV among the key players: major global telecoms, the ETSI standards body, the OPNFV project and solution providers. This page offers resources for you - the telecom executive, NFV architect, network operator, OpenStack developer, and OPNFV community.

Read the NFV Foundation Report

Using standard IT virtualization technology, NFV aims to consolidate many network equipment types on to industry-standard, high-volume servers, switches, and storage; making the networks more agile and efficient. In this report, OpenStack’s role in solutioning requirements and relationships with other NFV bodies. Major telecom providers and enterprises tell their stories - from PoC to production.
